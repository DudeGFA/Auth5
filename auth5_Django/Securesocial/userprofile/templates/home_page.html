Welcome to Decentralized Social

This app was created to serve as sample of Auth5 integration as a third party authentication system. It has web pages to view user profiles containing their Personal Identifiable information

To view A user's PII, you must either be the owner of the PII or be permitted by the PII owner on Auth5 to view that particular PII

The PII owner grants you permission using your Auth5 User Id

Click here to authenticate into decentralized social using Auth5

On successful login, you will be redirected to your profile on decentralized social

The values you see for the PII fields on your profile will be the data stored in your DWN.

This data is accessed using the DID and and recordIds you specify for each field on your Auth5 dashboard
