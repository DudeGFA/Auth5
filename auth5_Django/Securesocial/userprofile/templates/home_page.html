Welcome to Decentralized Social

This app was created as a sample of Auth5 integration, serving as a third-party authentication system. It includes web pages to view user profiles containing Personal Identifiable Information (PII).

To view a user's PII, you must either be the owner of the PII or be permitted by the PII owner on Auth5 to view that particular PII. The PII owner grants permission using your Auth5 User ID.

Click here to authenticate into Decentralized Social using Auth5

On successful login, you will be redirected to your profile on Decentralized Social.

The values you see for the PII fields on your profile will be the data stored in your DWN.

This data is accessed using the DID and and recordIds you specify for each field on your Auth5 dashboard
