Welcome to Decentralized Social

This app was created to serve as a sample of Auth5 integration. It has web pages to view user profiles containing their Personal Identifiable information

To view a user's PII, you must be permitted by the PII owner on Auth5 to view that particular PII

The PII owner grants you permission using your Auth5 User Id
